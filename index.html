FAQ

Find all the answers you need to enjoy our premium leather goods with confidence. Explore our FAQs to learn about our high-quality materials, easy returns, free shipping, and more.

How long do your leather bags last?

›
To make our leather bags, we use only the finest full grain leather, reinforced hardware and expert craftsmanship. With proper care, they'll age beautifully and only get better over the years. Many of our customers see them as heirlooms in the making.

Do you offer a warranty or guarantee?

›
Yes, all our leather bags come with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. We guarantee the quality of our materials and craftsmanship. If you experience any issues within this period, we'll repair or replace your bag at no cost.

Can I return or exchange my order?

›
We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original condition. Exchanges are subject to product availability. Please contact our customer service within 30 days of delivery to initiate a return or exchange.

How long does shipping take, and is it free?

›
We offer free standard shipping on all orders, which typically takes 3-5 business days within the US. International shipping takes 7-14 business days and may be subject to customs fees. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

How do I care for your leather bags?

›
Use a soft, dry cloth for regular cleaning. For deeper cleaning, use a leather conditioner specifically designed for full-grain leather. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or moisture. Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.

Will my device fit?

›
Each product page includes detailed dimensions and device compatibility information. Most of our bags can accommodate laptops up to 15 inches, tablets, and other devices. Check the specific product measurements for exact fit.

Will this bag fit me?

›
Our bags are designed to be comfortable for most body types. We provide detailed strap length and bag dimensions on each product page. If you're between sizes or have specific fit concerns, our customer service team can help you choose.

Is your checkout safe and secure?

›
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions and never store your payment information. Our checkout process is PCI-compliant and secured by the latest fraud prevention technologies.

How can I contact customer support?

›
Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST via email at support@realleatherco.com or by phone at (555) 123-4567. You can also use the live chat feature on our website during business hours.
